RESEARCH

I'm a macroeconomist interested in applications of text analysis in macroeconomics, machine learning, and expectations formation. My research interests primarily focus on investigating how text analysis through machine learning can be used in macroeconomics to provide cleaner causal inference.

I have authored or co-authored two publications on economic topics. A list of these appear below.
